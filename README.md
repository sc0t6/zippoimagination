# Zippo's Imagination
![](https://img.shields.io/github/downloads/sc0t6/zippoimagination/total)
![](https://img.shields.io/github/license/sc0t6/zippoimagination)

Broken Humour approved inside Minecraft Java to 1.20.x to 1.21.xx

> [!CAUTION]
> This mod is still in beta, which means that there will be bugs and some incompatiablities.
This mod provides what I have imagined and some stupid stuff that basically sucked in general but could also be funny for some people.

<details>
 <summary>Poop Mod Category</summary>
 
### Throwable Poop
- Throw poop at targets
- Inflicts Nausea effect on hit

### Placeable Poop
- Place poop on the ground
- Players walking through it will be slowed down
- Inflicts Nausea for 5 seconds

### Slimy Poop
- Drinkable poop variant
- Inflicts Poison and Nausea effects when consumed

### Player Poop
- By holding shift + p for 3 seconds, it poops out the placeable poop on the block behind you.

</details>


<details>
 <summary>Variation Ray Guns</summary>
 
### Shrink Ray
- Makes you 0.5 size
- Ability to make other players small too

### Grow Ray
- Makes you 1.5 size
- Ability to make other players big too

### Ray Gun
- A weapon (duh)
- Does incredible damage to people
- Requires ammo. To get it you need a nether star and 2 eye of enders.

### Normal Ray
- Obviously makes you in normal size.
 
 </details>

<details>
 <summary>Cameras</summary>

### Camera
- A normal camera
- You can take pictures and videos
- Ability to zoom far with the camera and use focus

### Security Cameras
- A camera you can place in houses, bases etc.
- Notifies you if theres player movement
- Able to place up to 7 before its the maximum

### Tablet
- Ability to see the pictures and videos you took from the camera
- Able to see the security cameras by linking them with this item
- Has "Play Sound" and "Switch Cameras". Of course you can move the cameras.

 </details>
