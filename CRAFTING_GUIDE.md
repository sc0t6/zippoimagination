## ğŸ“‹ Material Requirements Summary

### Common Materials
- **Iron Ingots** - Used in most recipes
- **Redstone** - Used in all electronic items
- **Blaze Rods** - Required for ray guns

### Rare Materials
- **Diamonds** - Shrink Ray, Ray Gun

- **Nether Star** - Ray Gun Ammo (16 per craft)
- **Eye of Ender** - Cameras, Ray Gun Ammo

- **Slime Balls** - Slimy Poop
- **Glass Panes** - Cameras

## ğŸ“– Recipe Book Integration

All recipes will appear in your Minecraft recipe book once you obtain the required ingredients. The recipes are discoverable in-game!

**How it works:**
1. Gather any ingredient (e.g., pick up dirt)
2. Open crafting table
3. Recipe book shows available recipes
4. Click to auto-fill crafting grid

## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Poop is Cheap!
Poop items use dirt and mushrooms - extremely cheap and renewable.

### Tip 2: Stock Up on Ammo
Each Ray Gun Ammo craft gives you **16 shots**. The Nether Star is expensive, so plan ahead!

### Tip 3: One Ray Gun is Enough
You only need ONE Ray Gun weapon. Just stock up on ammo!

### Tip 4: Camera Limit
You can only place **7 Security Cameras** per player. Choose locations wisely!

### Tip 5: Use /poopmenu in Creative
In creative mode, skip crafting entirely - use `/poopmenu` for instant access!
